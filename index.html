Meet the team
behind Orvyn

We are builders, researchers, and technologists building the simulation layer for Web3, AI, and the real world.

 

 

Orvyn is developed by a globally distributed team with backgrounds in system architecture, digital twins, and decentralized infrastructure.

 

From Singapore to Canada, our members have contributed to smart city projects, blockchain protocols, AI systems, and open-source research. We are united by the belief that simulations—when open, secure, and intelligent—can power better decisions across every domain.

We’re building Orvyn to enable autonomous agents to think before they act, DAOs to simulate before they vote, and developers to model complex environments before deploying code. Our team brings experience from institutions like GovTech Singapore, ST Engineering, Chainlink Labs, and leading AI research centers.

Join us in creating the foundation for a simulation-powered future. Whether you're a developer, data provider, or research group, Orvyn is your protocol playground.
